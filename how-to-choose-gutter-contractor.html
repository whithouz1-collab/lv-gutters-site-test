How do I choose a good gutter contractor near me?

Choose a gutter contractor who is licensed and insured, fabricates seamless gutters on-site, gives you a written price after actually looking at your home — not a number over the phone — backs the work with a real warranty, and has local reviews from your area. Those five things separate a pro from a truck-and-a-ladder operation.

Watch for the red flags: phone-only quotes, suspiciously low "gimmick" pricing that balloons once they're on-site, no proof of insurance, no written warranty, and pressure to sign before you've seen anything in writing. A cheap number that attracts you in is usually the most expensive mistake you can make.
