Do gutter guards actually work, or are they a scam?

Good gutter guards genuinely work — the "scam" reputation comes from cheap products and the oversold promise that you'll never touch your gutters again. A quality micro-mesh guard, installed at the right pitch, keeps leaves and pine needles out while letting water through, so your gutters stop clogging and overflowing.

The catch is that no guard is 100% maintenance-free, and a bad installation ruins even a great product — that's exactly where the bad stories come from. Three things decide whether a guard actually works: the quality of the product, a correct installation, and matching the guard to your debris. Florida oak leaves and pine needles slip right through a basic screen, so they need a finer micro-mesh to stop them.
